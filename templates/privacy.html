Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

Welcome to BookGenie. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and services.

Quick Summary: We do not sell your data. We only process information to provide better book recommendations, shopping features, and AI chatbot services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Catalog Data: The books displayed on our platform are stored in our MongoDB database.
  • User Account Data: When you register, we collect your username, email address, and encrypted password.
  • Cart and Order Data: Items added to cart and purchase history are stored securely for logged-in users.
  • Chat Conversations: Messages sent to the AI chatbot may be processed via OpenAI APIs to generate intelligent responses.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide book search and filtering features.
  • To manage your shopping cart and process orders.
  • To improve user experience and optimize database performance.
  • To generate AI-powered recommendations and responses through our chatbot.

3. Third-Party Services

We only share data with trusted service providers necessary to operate our platform:

  • OpenAI: Used only to process chatbot messages and generate AI responses.
  • MongoDB: Used to securely store book, user, cart, and order data.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures such as:

  • Encrypted passwords using secure hashing methods.
  • CORS protection to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Secure storage of sensitive API keys in environment (.env) files.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your account information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Contact us for any privacy-related concerns.
